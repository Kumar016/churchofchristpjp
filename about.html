Church Of Christ

Church of Christ is a community of believers committed to living out the message of Jesus in everyday life. Rooted in the Bible, we aim to reflect the love, truth, and unity taught in the New Testament. Our worship is simple and heartfelt, our fellowship is strong, and our mission is to serve others and glorify God in all we do. No matter where you’re from, you have a place here.

Our Purpose.

The purpose of the Church of Christ is to honor God by following the teachings of Jesus Christ, worshiping in spirit and truth, encouraging spiritual growth, and building a loving community rooted in the Bible.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to spread the gospel, serve others with compassion, strengthen the faith of believers, and lead lives that reflect Christ’s love and truth. We aim to be a light in the world and bring people closer to God.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We believe that our true identity and worth come from being followers of Jesus Christ. Through Him, we are made new, forgiven, and empowered to live with purpose. Knowing who we are in Christ helps us face life’s challenges with confidence and hope.

Church Is Family

The Church is not just a place we go—it is a family where we belong. We support, encourage, and care for one another, sharing life’s joys and struggles together. This sense of community helps us grow spiritually and strengthens our faith.

Kingdom Culture

We strive to live according to the values of God’s Kingdom. This means showing love, practicing justice, offering mercy, and walking humbly with God. Our goal is to reflect Jesus in our actions and attitudes, making a positive impact in the world around us.

Spirit Led Life

We rely on the Holy Spirit to guide and empower us every day. By being sensitive to the Spirit’s leading, we grow in wisdom, make godly choices, and bear spiritual fruit that honors God. Living a Spirit-led life keeps us connected to God’s purpose and power.


What We Believe.

The Bible is the Only Authority in Matters of Faith.

The Church of Christ believes that the Bible alone—specifically the New Testament—is the final authority in guiding all aspects of Christian life, worship, and church organization. There are no creeds, councils, or denominational doctrines used. Everything taught and practiced must have clear support from Scripture.

Salvation Comes Through Faith, Repentance, Confession, and Baptism.

Baptism is considered essential for salvation (Acts 2:38). It is practiced by full immersion in water as a response of faith and repentance—not merely as a symbol but as a step in becoming a Christian.

Worship Practices: Weekly Communion and A Cappella Singing.

In the Church of Christ, worship is modeled closely after the practices of the early Christians as described in the New Testament. Each Sunday, members gather to partake in the Lord’s Supper (Communion), following the example in Acts 20:7, where the early church met on the first day of the week to break bread in remembrance of Christ’s sacrifice.

Church Organization: One Body, Independent Congregations.

The Church of Christ teaches that the universal church is the one body of Christ, made up of all baptized believers, as described in Ephesians 4:4. However, each local congregation operates independently, without any central headquarters or denominational structure.
